Imitate

Originally I wanted to make an audio visualizer this week but I ended up being very overwhelmed so this is the result of what I was able to accomplish. So instead, I found this source that I wanted to play around with. I was always curious about the world and different data visualizations related to our planet. I got inspiration from this source here.

Integrate

After setting up the sketch inside the canvas, I played around with the settings such as the lighting and the strokes with all the objects printed. I wanted to see how I can adapt the code to make it my own and this was the result.

Innovate

I am most proud of the left and right arrow key functionality. I felt like it was more immersive having interactivity and control over the planet spinning instead of having it spin automatically. I also liked how the boxes now look like little marshmellows instead of being dark little cubes when lighting was enabled.

Reflection

Overall, I had fun messing around with the program and trying out the settings. I wish I had more time to experiment but this week has been very hectic for me so I tried my best with the time I had.
